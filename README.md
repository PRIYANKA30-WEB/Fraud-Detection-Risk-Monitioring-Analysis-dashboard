# Fraud-Detection-Risk-Monitioring-Analysis-dashboard

**Project Overview**

FraudShield is an interactive Fraud Detection and Risk Monitoring Dashboard developed using Power BI to identify suspicious transactions, monitor fraud patterns, analyze high-risk customers, and support fraud prevention strategies.
The dashboard helps financial institutions and businesses detect fraudulent behavior through transaction analysis, anomaly detection, customer risk segmentation, and fraud trend monitoring.

**Business Problem**
-Financial fraud causes major revenue loss, customer dissatisfaction, operational risks, and reputational damage.

**Businesses need:**
1.Real-time fraud monitoring
2.High-risk customer identification
3.Fraud trend analysis
4.Transaction anomaly detection
5.Risk-based fraud prevention strategies
6.This project provides a data-driven solution for fraud analysis and decision-making.


**Objectives**
-Analyze fraud trends across transactions
-Identify high-risk customers
-Detect suspicious transaction behavior
-Monitor international and late-night fraud
-Analyze fraud by merchant category and location
-Provide business recommendations for fraud prevention


**Tools & Technologies Used**

**Key Insights**
1.Fraud rate observed at approximately 4.87%.
2.International transactions contribute significantly to fraud activity.
3.Late-night transactions show increased fraud behavior.
4.High-risk customers account for major suspicious activity.
5.Merchant categories such as restaurants and ATM transactions show higher fraud exposure.
6.Location mismatches and abnormal transaction distances are strong fraud indicators.
